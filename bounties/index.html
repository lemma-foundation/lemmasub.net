Formal Conjectures

Bounties for harder Lean targets.

Lemma uses the public Formal Conjectures database from Google DeepMind as a source of exact Lean statements. A bounty is separate from the timed miner rounds and can be attempted by anyone, not only miners. Lemma is not affiliated with Google DeepMind in any way.

Source of targets

Formal Conjectures gives Lemma exact Lean statements to point at.

Formal Conjectures is a Google DeepMind project that collects mathematical statements written in Lean 4. Some entries are research solved: the mathematics is known, but a clean Lean proof artifact can still be valuable work.

That makes the bounty simple: pick one harder statement, publish it as the target, and reward a proof file that Lean accepts.

Formal Conjectures code is Apache 2.0. Other materials may be CC-BY 4.0 or covered by source-specific third-party licenses.
