Google DeepMind Formal Conjectures

Proof bounties for harder Lean targets.

Google DeepMind Formal Conjectures gives Lemma exact Lean statements to point at. A bounty is a separate reward for proving one selected statement when the target needs more time than a normal timed round.

What a bounty is

One selected statement. One Lean proof. One stated reward.

Normal Lemma rounds keep miners proving on a timer. Bounties are for harder targets: Lemma names the exact Lean statement, posts a reward, and accepts a miner's proof file if Lean verifies it against that statement.

Source of targets

Formal Conjectures gives Lemma exact Lean statements to point at.

Google DeepMind Formal Conjectures is a collection of mathematical statements written in Lean 4. Some entries are research solved: the mathematics is known, but a clean Lean proof artifact can still be valuable work.

That makes the bounty easy to understand: Lemma can pick one harder statement, publish it as the target, and reward a proof file that Lean accepts.
